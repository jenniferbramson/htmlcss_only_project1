Family Murder Mystery Dinner



A couple months ago, my family decided to celebrate my grandpa's birthday with a Roman murder mystery dinner. He was born and Italy and is obsessed with Augustus Caesar, so a Roman theme seemed perfect. Each person was given a sheet of paper explaining their character and all they knew a couple days beforehand. We arrived dressed in togas, adorned with props, and carrying Roman imitation food. We did not know who would be murdered - until the lights flickered off and my cousin Matt fell to the ground. We each started with some Roman coins. I was a Roman weapons and potions dealer and interchangeably spread rumors and kept my knowledge private in exchange for coin. Babies were born, secret lineage was revealed, coins were stolen, and weapons were broken, until only one of us suspected the murderer: Lindsay.
